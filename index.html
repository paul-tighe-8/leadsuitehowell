QUESTIONS

Common questions before you book

Is this just a chatbot? +

No. LeadSuite is a lead follow-up system. Chat is one piece. Calls, forms, SMS, booking, CRM organization, and reporting all sit on top of the same workflow so leads do not slip between tools.

Will this replace my sales team? +

No. LeadSuite handles the parts that wear sales teams down: instant response, qualification, scheduling, and CRM updates. Your team takes the conversations that need a human, with cleaner context.

Can this work with my existing CRM? +

In most cases yes. We'll review your current CRM and lead sources during the call and show how LeadSuite would fit, replace, or sit alongside what you already use.

Can it handle website and Meta leads? +

Yes. LeadSuite captures leads from your website forms, Meta lead ads, chat, and phone calls, and routes them through one consistent follow-up process.

Can it help with missed calls? +

Yes. Missed calls trigger an instant text or callback so the lead does not contact your competitor in the next minute.

What happens after I book a session? +

A real team member at Howell Studios reviews your answers, looks at your current setup, and walks you through your follow-up gaps on the call. You leave with a clearer next step, even if LeadSuite is not the right fit.

How fast can LeadSuite be set up? +

It depends on the scope. Many teams have core lead capture, follow-up, and booking running quickly. We'll give you a realistic timeline once we see your setup.

Will I be pressured into buying? +

No. The session is a practical review. If LeadSuite is not the right fit, we will tell you. If it is, we will explain how it would work for your business and let you decide from there.
